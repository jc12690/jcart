Privacy Policy

Retrac Labs · Last updated: May 1, 2026

At Retrac Labs, we believe privacy is a right, not a feature. This page describes the privacy practices for jarredmcarter.com and all apps published under the Retrac Labs name.

[01] Website
jarredmcarter.com

Nothing.

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics software, or any form of user tracking. We do not collect names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any other personal information. No data is stored, sold, or shared with third parties.

[02] macOS App
SnippyStack

Nothing.

SnippyStack stores your clipboard history exclusively on your own Mac using Apple's standard local storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device.

  • Does not connect to the internet
  • Does not transmit data to Retrac Labs or any third party
  • Does not require an account or sign-in
  • Does not use analytics or crash reporting
  • Does not display advertising

You are in full control of your data at all times. You can view, pin, delete, or clear your entire clipboard history from within the app at any time.

[03] Your Rights

Because we collect no data, there is nothing to access, correct, export, or delete on our end. Your data lives on your device and is entirely under your control.

[04] Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page and revise the date at the top. We will never introduce data collection without clearly disclosing it here first.
